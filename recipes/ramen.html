Ramen Recipe

This is Ramen...

Ramen is a very popular dish in which you maeke noodles combined with flavoring and it's cheap and very accessible worldwide

Ingredients...

  • Ramen
  • Bowl
  • Water
  • Microwave

Steps

  1. Mix water in bowl with ramen
  2. Put in microwave for 3 minutes
  3. Then strain and put back in bowl and then mix ramen packet and voila you're done!
